IP Telephony
The telephone signal is digitized through the gateway, compressed, distributed to packets and sent over the IP network in the desired direction using the TCP / IP protocol. The signal then passes through another gateway and is converted into a telephone signal.
  • Includes all 404 and 525 numbers
  • Subscription fee for individual customers is 4 AZN
  • Subscription fee for corporate customers is 7 AZN
  • The connection fee is free. (Opening fee - State fee 30 AZN)
  • If the selected number is taken, an additional fee is applied.
  • Sip Trunk service
  • Providing additional channels over the phone
  • Transmission of E1 Channel
  • For all numbers that are not paid within 3 months, the existing contract is canceled and added to the list of resale.
